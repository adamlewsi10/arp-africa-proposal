The Client

ARP Africa Travel is East Africa's leading destination management group, operating through Pollman's Tours & Safaris in Kenya and Ranger Safaris in Tanzania — with a combined heritage of over 110 years. Backed by Alterra Capital Partners, with a head office in Harrow managing B2B relationships with travel agents worldwide, and over 200 owned vehicles across East Africa, ARP is embarking on a technology transformation to match its operational scale and ambition.

The Challenge
🔍

Repeat Customers Are Invisible

No way to identify when the same traveller books through different agents or returns for a second safari.

📋

Complaints Can't Be Traced

Issues arrive via agent, head office, or on-site — with no link between them and no pattern visibility.

📊

Driver Performance Is a Blind Spot

Six complaints about the same driver? Right now, nobody would know.

📁

Everything Lives in Spreadsheets

Three entities, three sets of spreadsheets, no connected view of agents, customers, or suppliers.

🔗

Bookings Don't Link Together

Safari and beach extensions are booked separately — no connected customer journey across the group.

Desired Outcomes
🌍

One CRM for Three Entities

Agents, end-customers, and suppliers managed in a single system across Harrow, Kenya, and Tanzania.

🎫

Centralised Complaint Tracking

Every complaint — whether from agent, head office, or on-location — linked to one customer record.

📈

Operational Performance Reporting

Spot patterns in driver complaints, service issues, and agent satisfaction with real data.

🤝

Agent Intelligence at a Glance

See which agents book Kenya, Tanzania, or both — and their repeat customer behaviour.

✈️

Connected Customer Journeys

A complete view of every traveller — where they've been, what they booked, and how they rated it.
